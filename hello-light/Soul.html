Soul Stones

Category:2D Animation

Client: Vancouver Film School

Date: 2016

Role Script, Illustration, Music Composition , 2D animation, video edition



Inspired by two of my passions (video games, and fairy tales), I created ‘Soul Stones’ a prologue for a fictitious fantasy video game. Soul Stones is a magical story, set a long time ago in the Land of Illusions, where the feelings of a beautiful princess are stolen by a dark wizard and put into seven soul stones. The story is a metaphor for the search inside ourselves for our hidden feelings.
