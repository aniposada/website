WONDER

Category:2D Animation

Client: Science World at Telus World of Science

Date: 2017

In collaboration with Rethink Vancouver

I used all my creativity and my 2D animations skills on After Effects to bring to life the animals of WONDER, a new gallery at Science World at TELUS World of Science in Vancouver for children between 0-5 years old. The animation is a commercial video to promote and invited parents and kids to the new gallery.
